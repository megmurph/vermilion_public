# One-pager: fundraise messaging

Resolves the **"Template"** cell in the sheet's **Funding announcement → Messaging** section: *Draft one-page "who we are, why we matter, why now" internal doc.*

**Live source:** https://app.notion.com/p/38070e83e94d80a99873e3c8e632fbcc

The money is validation, not the story. This one-pager forces the real narrative — problem, approach, proof, timing — and gives every channel a single set of facts to get right. Snapshot below in case Notion isn't reachable.

**Company:** [Name] · **Announcement date:** [Date, time, timezone]
**Status:** [Draft / In review / Locked — embargoed until ___]

### The raise (the facts everyone must get right)

- **Amount & round:** [$X Series A / Seed]
- **Lead investor:** [Firm] · **Participating:** [Firms / angels]
- **Total raised to date:** [$X] · **Valuation:** [disclose / do not disclose]
- **Board / notable adds:** [Name, role]

### Who we are

*One clean paragraph. Category, what we do, and who's behind it. A reporter should be able to lift this verbatim.*

> [e.g. "Northwall builds developer infrastructure for typed, cross-language service communication. Founded in 2023 by Mara Quinn and Dev Patel, its open-source framework, Pylon, gets 10k downloads a month."]

- **The one-liner:** [the category-defining sentence]
- **Founder credibility:** [Prior companies, domain depth, why these people]

### Why we matter

*The reason this raise is news beyond the dollar figure. Problem → approach → proof. The money is validation, not the story.*

- **The problem:** [What's broken and who feels it.]
- **What's different about us:** [The wedge — not features, the insight.]
- **Proof it's working:** [Traction metric, customers, growth rate, retention.]
- **Why this investor backed us:** [The thesis in the lead's words — borrow their credibility.]

### Why now

*Timing is the whole pitch of a raise. Market shift + company inflection + what the capital unlocks.*

- **Market inflection:** [The trend making this urgent today, not last year.]
- **Company inflection:** [The milestone that made now the moment to scale.]
- **What the money does:** [Hiring, GTM, R&D — concrete, 2–3 items.]
- **The 12-month bet:** [Where we'll be by next year if this works.]

### Spokesperson quote

*One quotable line from the founder, one from the lead investor. Conversational, not corporate.*

> Investor: ["..."]
